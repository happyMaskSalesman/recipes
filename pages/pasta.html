Pasta and Onions

Description

Servings: 3-4
Total time: 20 minutes

An easy, delicious, and affordable dinner. Simple yet packed full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1lb pasta of your choice
  • 4 large white or yellow onions
  • Basil
  • Parmasan

Steps

  1. Dice up the onions to your preferred size, add to a large skillet with a light coating of olive oil over medium heat. Continue cooking until onions have caramelized and turned dark brown, stirring regularly.
  2. Boil a pot of water, and cook pasta for the recommended time. Drain after cooking
  3. Once onions and pasta are finished cooking, combine both in a large pot. Add basil, olive oil, and parmesan cheese to taste. Stir thoroughly.
  4. That's it! Enjoy!
