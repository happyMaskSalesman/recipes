Healthy Smoothie

Description

Servings: 1-2
Total time: 10 minutes

The detox classic. Enjoy on a daily basis to get your servings of fruits and vegetables and to contribute to a healthy lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup blueberries
  • 1/4 cup raspberries
  • 1/4 cup diced mangoes
  • 1 pitted apple
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Combine all ingretients in a large blender.
  2. Blend on high for one minute, or until smooth.
  3. Enjoy!
